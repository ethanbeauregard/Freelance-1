Our Establishment

Come in and view Carol's many original oil on canva paintings. They are all hanging on exposed brick walls. You could almost walk into one of these large bold color paintings, some are over 8 feet across. We have a warm friendly atmosphere. Sit and visit with old friends, make new friends. This is a great place to hold your meetings. We host small bridal, wedding, anniversary & bady showers. We are your hometown luch spot and coffee shop serving the best coffee around!

Entertainment

Food and Beverages aren't the only reason to come to Carol's!

Live Music

Most days the sound of music will flow out of the doors at Carol's. Many local musicians showcase their talent and create a melodic atmosphere that pairs nicely with the delicious food, coffee, and bright artwork.

Open Mic Night

If you're a musician and looking for a place to show your talent on Thursday nights, stop by Carol's for Open Mic Night!

Thursday Nights: 5pm Sign Ups - 6pm Show Time

Hosted by: Greg Neff

Art Classes

Every third Wednesday of the month Carol shares her love of art through teaching painting classes.

Painting classes are open to anyone with or without art experience. If you're interested ask about signing up!

Classes: $35/person

Artist Of The Month

Every month Carol's has an artist of the month who displays their work inside.
